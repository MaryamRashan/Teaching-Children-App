Abu Bakr as Siddiq reported: "I requested the Messenger of Allaah to teach me a supplication which I could recite in my Salat (prayer). Thereupon he said, recite,

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِي ظُلْمََ كَثِيرً ، وَلَا يَغْفِر آلذُنُوبَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ ، فَاغْفِر لِي مَغْفِرَةً مِنْ عِنْدِكَ ، وَارْهَمْنِي ، إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْغفُورُ الرَّحِيمُ

O Allaah! I have considerably wronged myself. There is none to forgive the sins but you. So grant me pardon and have mercy on me. You are the Most Forgiving, the Most Compassionate.
Sahih al Bukhari and Muslim
