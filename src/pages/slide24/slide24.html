Anas reported:

The Messenger of Allaah said, "Recite frequently,

يَا ذَا الْجَلاَلِ وَالإِكْرَامِ

Ya Dhal Jalaali wal Ikram

O Possesor of Glory and Honour.
at Tirmidhi

This Hadith tells us that one should make abundant use of the words 'Ya Dhal Jalaali wal Ikram' in his prayer because these words contain the Praise of Allaah and His Perfect Attributes.

Some have said that al Jalaal and al Ikram are Allaah's Greatest Names.
