Imran bin Husain reported:

The Prophet taught my father two statements to recite in his Dua. These are,

اللَّهُمَّ أَلْهِمْنِي رُشْدِي، وَأَعِذْنِي مِنْ شَرِّ نَفْسِي

Allahumma al himni rushdi, wa a'idhni min sharri nafsi

O Allah! Inspire in me guidance and deliver me from the evils within myself.
at Tirmidhi
