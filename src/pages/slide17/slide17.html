O Allaah, make my religion easy for me by virtue of which my affairs are protected, set right for me my world where my life exists, make good for me my Hereafter which is my resort to which I have to return, and make my life prone to perform all types of good, and make death a comfort for me from evil.
Sahih Muslim

This is a very comprehensive prayer which simply means, "O Allaah, Help me spend all my life in complete devotion to You, doing what You like and avoiding what You dislike."
