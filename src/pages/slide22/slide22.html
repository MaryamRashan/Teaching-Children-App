Ali reported: A slave who had made a contract with his master to pay for his freedom, came to me and said: "I am unable to fulfill my obligation, so help me."

He said to him: "Shall I not teach you a supplication which the Messenger of Allaah taught me? It will surely prove so effective that if you have a debt as large as a huge mountain, Allaah will surely pay it for you. Say:

اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِي بِحَلاَلِكَ عَنْ حَرَامِكَ، وَأَغْنِنِي بِفَضْلِكَ عَمَّن سِوَاكَ

Allaahumm-akfini bihalalika an haramika, wa aghnini bifadlika amman siwaka

O Allaah! Grant me enough of what You make lawful so that I may dispense with what You make unlawful, and enable me by Your Grace to dispense with all but You

It is important to recite this Dua in order to seek Allaah's Help in paying one's debts and in avoiding to beg of people.
