Abu Hurairah reported:- The Messenger of Allaah used to supplicate:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُذُبِكَ مِنَ الْجُوعِ ، فَإِنَّهُ بِئْسَ الضَّجِيعُ ، وَأَعُوذُبِكَ مِنَ الْخِيَانَةِ ، فَإِنَّهَا بِئْسَتِ الْبِطَانَةُ

O Allaah! I seek refuge in You from hunger; surely, it is the worst companion. And I seek refuge in You from treachery; surely it is a bad inner trait.
Abu Dawud
