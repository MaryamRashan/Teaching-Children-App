Allaahumma inni as'aluka min khairi ma sa'alaka minhu nabiyyuka Muhammadan salallahu alaihi wa sallam. Wa a'udhu bika min sharri mas ta'adha minhu nabiyyuka Muhammadan salallahu alaihi wa sallam. Wa antal musta'anu, wa alaikal balaghu, wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah.

O Allaah, I beg of You the good which Your Prophet Muhammad begged of You; and I seek refuge in You from the evil where from Your Prophet Muhammad sought refuge. You are the One from Whom help is sought and Yours is the responsibility to communicate (the truth). There is no power or strength except with Allaah the Exalted, the Great.
at Tirmidhi
