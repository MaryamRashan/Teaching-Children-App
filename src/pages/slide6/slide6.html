Ali reported:

The Messenger of Allaah said to me, "Recite:

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي ، وَسَدِّدْنِي

Allahumma hdini wa saddidni

O Allaah! Direct me to the Right Path and make me adhere to the Straight Path.

In another narration,

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْهُدَى ، وَالسَّدَادَ

Allahumma inni as'aluk alhuda was sadad

O Allaah! I beg you for gudiance and uprightness.
