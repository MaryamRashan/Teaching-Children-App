Abdullah bin Umar reported: The Messenger of Allaah used to supplicate thus,

‏اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِن زَوَالِ نِعْمَتِكَ، وَتَحَوُّلِ عَافِيَتِكَ، وَفُجَاءَةِ نِقْمَتِكَ، وَجَمِيعِ سَخَطِكَ

O Allaah! I seek refuge in You against the declining of Your Favours, passing of safety, the suddenness of Your punishment and all that which displeases You.
Sahih Muslim

This is also a very comprehensive prayer. Allaah has blessed man with innumerable gifts. One becomes conscious of them when one is deprived of any of tem, hence it is said that the value of a blessing is realized at its loss. For eyes, ears, tongue, health, property, children and such countless blessings, we owe gratitude to Allaah. In the prayer cited in this Hadith, one entreats Allaah not to deprive us of any blessing given to him. 'Aafiyah means safe from disease grief and troubles.
