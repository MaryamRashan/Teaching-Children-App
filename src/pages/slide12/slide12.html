Tariq bin Ashyam reported:-
A man came to the Prophet and said to him, "O Messenger of Allaah! What shall I say if I want to pray to my Rabb?"
He said, "Say,

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْلِي ، وَرْحَمْنِي ، وَ عَافِنِي ، وَرْزُقْنِي

Allaahhummaghfir li, warhamni, wa aafini, warzuqni

O Allaah! Forgive me, have mercy on me, protect me and provide me wit sustenance.
Surely this supplication is better for you in this life and in the Hereafter."
Sahih Muslim
