Allaah the Exalted says, "And your Rabb said, "Invoke me, (i.e. believe in My Oneness) (and ask Me for anything) I will respond to your invocation. Surah Ghafir v.60

An Nu'man bin Bashir reported:
The Prophet said, "Dua (supplication) is worship."
Abu Dawud

Aishah reported: The Messenger of Allaah liked comprehensive supplications or Al Jawami(i.e. supplications with very few words but comprehensive in meanings), and discarded others. Abu Dawud

All Hadith have been taken from the Englih translation of the Riyadh us Saliheen compiled by Al Imaam Abu Zakariya Yahya bin Sharaf an Nawawi ad Dimashqi
