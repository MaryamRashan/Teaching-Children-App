Ibn Abbas said: The Messenger of Allaah used to say when he was in distress,

لّاإِلَه إِلَّا اللَّهُ الْعَظِيمُ الْحَلِيمُ ، لَا إِلَهَ إَلَّا اللَّهُ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمُ، لَا إِلَهَ إلَّا اللَّهُ رَبُّ السَمَاواتِ وَرَبُّ الأَرْضِ، وَرَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْكَرِيْمِ

None has the right to be worshipped but Allaah the Incomparably Great, the Compassionate. None has the right to be worshipped but Allaah the Rabb of the Mighty Throne. None has the right to be worshipped but Allaah the Rabb of the heavens, the Rabb of the earth, and the Rabb of the Honourable Throne.
Sahih Bukhari & Muslim
