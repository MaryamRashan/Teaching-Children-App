Aishah reported: The Prophet used to supplicate (in these words).

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا عَمِلْتُ وَ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا لَمْ أَعْمَلْ

Allahumma inni audhu bika min sharri ma amiltu, wa min sharri ma lam a'mal

O Allaah! I seek refuge in you from the evil of that which I have done and the evil of that which I have not done.
