Allaahumma inni a'udhu bika minal 'ajzi wal kasali, wal bukhli wal harami, wa 'adhabil qabri. Allaahumma ati nafsi taqwaha, wa zakkiha Anta khairu man zakkaha, Anta waliyyuha wa maulaha.
Allaahumma inni a'udhu bika min ilmin la yanfau, wa min qalbin la yakhsha'u, wa min nafsin la tashba'u, wa min da'watin la yustajabu laha.

O Allaah! I seek refuge in You from the inability (to do good), indolence, cowardice, miserliness, decrepitude (weakness) and torment of the grave.

O Allaah! Grant me the sense of piety and purify my soul as You are the Best to purify it. You are its Guardian and its Protecting Friend.

Allaah! I seek refuge in You from the knowledge which is not beneficial, and from a heart which does not fear (You), and from desire which is not satisfied, and from prayer which is not answered
